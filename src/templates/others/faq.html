Account and commenting help

Q: I have forgotten my password for my Guardian registration account.

A: To reset your password please follow this link. You will receive an email with further instructions. Do check your spam folder if it doesn’t arrive in your inbox.

Please note, this email should arrive within a few seconds. If it does not, then it could be because your email provider quarantines automated emails to scan for viruses for a short period before sending them to you, in which case you will need to contact your IT team or email provider. If the registration email does not arrive at all, the most likely reason is that the email address that you have entered is not registered on our system.

Q: I want to change my username.

A: You will be able to change your username if you see an option to do so at this link. You can change your username name once. So please think carefully before creating your username. Also, at this time it is not possible to change usernames for GuardianWitness profiles.

Q: I want to change my email address.

A: Go to this link and sign in if you’ve not already done so. Amend the email address and press ‘Save changes’ at the bottom of the page. An email with the subject line ‘Please confirm your email address change’ will be sent to the new email address. Select the ‘Click to Confirm’ link within the email to confirm the change. You should be taken to a page with the heading ‘Success! Your email address has been updated’.

Q: I want to change my registered email address/postal address/other personal details.

A: Please follow this link and sign in if you’ve not already done so, amend the details and press ‘Save changes’ at the bottom of the page.

Q: I want to unsubscribe from emails.

A: All of our email services have an ‘unsubscribe’ link at the end of the email. Clicking this will provide an option to unsubscribe from the particular email or unsubscribe from all emails. It should be noted that Guardian Jobs emails and Guardian newsletters are sent from different systems, as a result, you can unsubscribe from all in one system and still receive emails from the other. Should this happen, you can just use the unsubscribe link on the other email service.

If you have a registration account, you can adjust your newsletter preferences by following this link.

Should this fail to stop the emails, please contact us here at user support (the address is at the bottom of this page) for newsletters or the Jobs Help team for job alerts and attach one of the unwanted emails including the header so we can investigate.

Q: How do I register to post comments?

  1. Go to http://www.theguardian.com and click on “Sign in” in the top-left corner of the screen. You will be taken to the ‘Sign in with the Guardian’ page.
  2. If you have not previously registered, on ‘Don’t have an account? Sign up’, click on ‘Sign up’ to create your account.
  3. Complete the form and click on the ‘Create account’ button. If creating your account with Google or Facebook, please note that your Google or Facebook profile name will become your username.
  4. You will receive an email to validate your email address. After you have validated your email address you will be able to comment.

Q: I would like to add a link to my comment.

Before posting your comment, to insert a link, highlight the text in your comment that you wish to turn into a link, then click on the ‘Link” button and you will be prompted to enter the web address after “http://www.”

Q: I want one of my comments deleted.

A: You need to contact the moderation team, the quickest way to do this is to use the ‘report’ button stating that you’re the author of the comment and why you want it to be removed. Abuse reports reach the moderation team instantly and your comments will be taken down immediately where appropriate. For contact details for the moderation team, please see the answer to the next question.

Q: Why have I been banned/put on a pre-moderation list?

A: All moderation queries are handled by the moderation team and should be sent to them directly at moderation@theguardian.com or opinion.moderation@theguardian.com (for moderation on Opinion articles specifically). There’s also an extensive list of frequently asked questions about community features and functionality on the Guardian website.

Q: How do I delete my account?

A: It’s possible to delete your account by going to:

profile.theguardian.com/delete

and following the online instructions.

General Queries

Q: How do I contact…?

A: We have a contacts page which is accessible from the bottom of all of our web pages. Please note that if somebody is a freelance writer, they will not have an @theguardian.com email address. If this is so, you should contact the editorial desk that they contribute to and ask for your message to be passed on.

Q: I’ve spotted a mistake or wish to complain about something in the Guardian.

A: It is the policy of the Guardian to correct significant errors as soon as possible and the paper has appointed a readers’ editor to deal with questions and complaints from readers, you can email them at guardian.readers@theguardian.com for the Guardian or observer.readers@observer.co.uk

For less significant errors such as spelling mistakes or broken links, please scroll to the bottom of the Guardian page where the spelling mistake or broken link appears, click/tap on ‘help’ and follow the link to ‘Contact our user support team’. This will take you to an online form which you can complete, we will then investigate and get back to you as soon as possible.

If you can include a link to the article that contains the mistake, that would be very useful.

Q: I’m interested in working for or having work experience with the Guardian.

A: The Guardian’s work for us page has details of all of our job vacancies and information about how to apply for work experience.

Q: I have an idea for an article.

A: You need to pitch your idea to the most appropriate section. Please read our contributors’ guide for more information.

Q: How do I seek permission to reproduce an article or photograph from the Guardian or Observer?

A: It is free to link to a Guardian website article, but if you wish to reproduce material you need to seek permission from our syndication department, you can do this by clicking on the ‘reuse this content’ link at the bottom of each article. For further information please visit our syndication page.

Q: How do I advertise in the Guardian?

A: Please see our advertising page for information and contact details.

Q: When will you be publishing the 2018 Football World Cup guide and wallchart in the print edition?

A: The football World Cup guide will be published in the Guardian on Saturday 9 June. The wallchart will be published in the following day’s Observer, on Sunday 10 June.

Q: How do I contact user support?

A: Please check this FAQ page as your question may have already been answered. If not, please scroll to the bottom of the Guardian page where the problem is happening (if you’re not reporting a problem, scroll down to the bottom of any Guardian web page), click/tap on ‘help’ then ‘contact our user support team’ and complete and submit the online form. We’ll then get back to you as soon as possible. You can also follow us on Twitter at @GuardianHelp
