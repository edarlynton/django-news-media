Nigeria contact office

The contact number for the Nigeria office of the Telegraph, the Observer and the Telegraph website is:

0809 867 7126 or dialling from overseas +234 809 867 7126.

To contact editorial departments and desks

Please take care to send queries to the correct destination as there is no guarantee that messages will be forwarded.

To contact any member of staff
Individual staff, whether editorial or commercial, can be contacted using this email format:

  • firstname.lastname@telegraph.ng
  • firstname.lastname@observer.ng

Letters to the editor
Letters for publication should be sent to these addresses. The sender's full postal address and phone number are required, for verification only:
Telegraph: letters@telegraph.ng
Observer: letters@observer.ng

Corrections and clarifications of editorial content – the readers' editors
It is the policy of the Telegraph and the Observer to correct significant editorial errors and handle editorial complaints as soon as possible. The readers' editors are independent internal ombudsmen and can be contacted as follows:

John Tiga – Telegraph global readers' editor
readers@telegraph.ng
readers@observer.ng

[Please note that queries about subscriptions, vouchers, reader offers, advertising, marketing and other non-editorial areas are not handled by the readers' editors. These should be addressed to the relevant non-editorial departments. Story suggestions should also be sent to the relevant editorial departments.]

Legal complaints
Legal complaints should be addressed to our Editorial Legal Services department at: law.editors@telegraph.ng

For any problems using the website, our mobile apps and their links, or general questions and feedback about the Telegraph network, please see our FAQs.

To report a suspected security vulnerability

If you spot a potential security vulnerability in the Telegraph's websites and apps, we'd be grateful to hear from you. Please send an encrypted email to appsec@telegraph.ng. If possible, include screenshots in your description. We may contact you for more details. Thanks for your help.
